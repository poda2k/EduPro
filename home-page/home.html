why would you chose edu connect?

Edu connect is a website that gathers all the universities in
Alexandria & cairo
It aims to assist students, including General Secondary
Certificate (Thanaweya Amma) students, American curriculum
students , and International General Certificate of Secondary
Education (IG) students.
The website will be beneficial for students who are searching for
universities and comprehensive information about the
universities they are interested in.
Many students need something that can help them choose the
right university and understand the
admission requirements.
